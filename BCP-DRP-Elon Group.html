Implementation Timeline

Phase 1: Assessment & Planning (Months 1-3)

Month 1: Establish governance structure, conduct enterprise risk assessment, initiate stakeholder engagement

Month 2: Complete business impact analysis, identify critical dependencies, develop recovery strategies

Month 3: Finalize project plan, secure budget approval, launch communication campaign

Phase 2: BCP/DRP Development (Months 4-8)

Months 4-5: Develop enterprise BCP framework, create department plan templates, design IT disaster recovery architecture

Months 6-7: Facilitate department plan development, implement technology solutions, develop crisis management procedures

Month 8: Integrate department plans, finalize documentation, prepare training materials

Phase 3: Implementation & Training (Months 9-11)

Month 9: Deploy technology infrastructure, launch training programs, implement notification systems

Month 10: Conduct coordinator training, complete employee awareness, establish alternate sites

Month 11: Finalize vendor agreements, complete procedure documentation, prepare for testing phase

Phase 4: Testing & Refinement (Month 12)

Month 12: Execute testing program, conduct gap analysis, implement improvements, transition to maintenance phase

Ongoing: Maintenance and continuous improvement cycles
